🃏 Biriba (Μπιρίμπα) Rules

📋 Overview

Biriba (Μπιρίμπα) is a popular Greek rummy-style card game played with 2 decks (108 cards including 4 Jokers). Players form melds (sets and runs) to score points. The first team to exceed the target score wins.

🎯 Card Values

🃏 Joker20 pts
A Ace15 pts
2 Two10 pts
8-K High10 pts
3-7 Low5 pts

🏆 Biriba Bonuses

A Biriba is a meld of 7+ cards. A clean biriba has no wild cards. A dirty biriba contains a wild card (Joker or 2).

TypeCleanDirty
Run (7-12 cards, plain suit)200100

📊 Other Scoring

  • Going out bonus: +100 points
  • Not picking up biribaki: −100 points penalty
  • Cards in hand at end: Negative points (counted against you)
  • Cards melded on table: Positive points (scored for you)

🎮 How to Use This App

  1. Create a new game with team names and target score
  2. After each round, click "Add Round" to enter scores
  3. Enter melded card points, cards-in-hand penalty, biriba bonuses, etc.
  4. The app tracks cumulative scores and declares the winner automatically
